Storage organisation & safety

A few minutes of organising prevents serious injuries

1
Heavy items low, light items high
Prevents toppling and makes retrieval safer for everyone
2
Never exceed shelf weight limits
Overloaded shelves can collapse without warning
3
Use a step stool — never climb shelves
Falls are a leading cause of workplace injuries
4
Keep aisles and exits clear
Blocked paths cause trips and slow emergency evacuations
5
Inspect shelves regularly
Check for damage, loose fittings, rust, or instability
